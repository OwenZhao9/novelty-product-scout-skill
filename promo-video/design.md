# Novelty Product Scout Promo Design

## Brand Role

宣传视频用于展示“新奇特选品雷达”这个电商 skill。观众是比赛评委、电商卖家、选品团队和对 AI skill 有判断力的用户。画面要像一个真实可用的选品情报系统，不像模板广告。

## Visual Mood

- 深色科技感仪表盘
- 精密、可信、数据驱动
- 有商业工具质感，不做夸张赛博朋克
- 视觉重点是“从公开信号到可测试候选品”的流程闭环

## Palette

- Background: `#080d11`
- Panel: `#0f161b`
- Field: `#0b1318`
- Foreground: `#edf7f4`
- Muted text: `#8fa09d`
- Line: `rgba(179, 214, 205, 0.16)`
- Green accent: `#4ce0a2`
- Cyan accent: `#63d8ff`
- Amber accent: `#f2b84b`
- Danger accent: `#ff6f7d`

## Typography

- Display / English: `Space Grotesk`, 800-900 weight
- Chinese UI text: `Noto Sans JP`, 600-900 weight
- Data / metadata: `IBM Plex Mono`, 500-800 weight
- Use large readable video type: headings 72-118px, body 28-38px, labels 18-26px.

## Shape And Layout

- 16:9 landscape, 1920 x 1080.
- Rounded rectangles: 8px radius or less.
- Use panels, data cards, hairline dividers, signal chips, and device-frame screenshots.
- Keep content anchored to edges with split-frame compositions.
- Never use floating marketing hero cards as the whole scene.

## Motion

- Rhythm: slow-build → BUILD → PEAK → breathe → CTA.
- Primary transition: velocity-matched push/blur.
- Accent transition: chromatic scan wipe for the hero result reveal.
- Motion should imply signal flow: chips converge, bars fill, ranking rows lock in, links branch out.

## Do Not Do

- Do not overpromise “真实销量” where the tool uses公开信号/估算信号.
- Do not use fake marketplace logos as official endorsements.
- Do not use purple/blue one-note gradients.
- Do not make the video look like a landing page.
